## Parenthesization

Add parenthesis to make evaluation order more explicit, e.g.

`2 + 2 * 3 - 3` will produce the same result as: `2 + (2 * 3) - 3`

### Expressions to solve

1. 2 + 2 * 3 - 1
2. (^) 10 $ 1 + 1
3. 2 ^ 2 * 4 ^ 5 + 1

### Solutions

1. (2 + (2 * 3)) - 1
2. (^) 10 (1 + 1)
3. ((2 ^ 2) * (4 ^ 5)) + 1
